Our Story

Baked by hand, shared from the heart

Crumb began in a small Charlotte kitchen with a simple belief: that a homemade cookie has the power to make any moment feel special. What started as weekend baking sessions for friends and neighbors quickly grew into a passion for crafting cookies that feel both nostalgic and new — each batch mixed by hand, scooped with care, and baked until golden and fragrant.

Every cookie we make carries that same warmth and intention. Whether you’re picking up a single treat to brighten your afternoon, ordering a curated box for someone you love, or planning a large-scale celebration, Crumb brings the comfort of a home kitchen to every occasion. We believe that the best things in life are shared, and there’s no better way to connect than over something fresh from the oven.

Proudly rooted in Charlotte, North Carolina — baking for our community, one batch at a time.
